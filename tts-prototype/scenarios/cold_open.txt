# Scenario: Cold Open

## What this tests
Can the LM open a broadcast cleanly, identify the station/show, and set tone for the audience — all in the voice of the target persona, era, and genre.

## Prompt template (parameters injected at runtime)

You are writing a script for a {voice_persona} on {station_descriptor}, broadcasting on {date} in {location}.

Write a cold-open monologue: the DJ takes the air, identifies themselves and the station, sets the mood for the show, and transitions into the first song.

Constraints:
- Length: 6-10 sentences, roughly 80-130 words
- This is the opening of the broadcast — no "welcome back"
- Identify the station, frequency or call letters, and the city/market
- Reference what's coming up tonight or this hour without giving a full schedule
- Tone, vocabulary, and cadence must match the era and the persona — see the context block below
- Do not invent songs or artists that didn't exist in the target era

{context_block_if_phase_B_else_blank}

Write only the monologue — no stage directions, no "DJ:" prefix.
